A professional-grade micro-stamping device designed for targeted at-home use. Key Benefits

  • Increases serum absorption by up to 40× vs surface application
  • Creates controlled micro-channels to stimulate collagen
  • Designed for targeted use on specific areas (not full rolling)
  • Reduces appearance of scars, pores, and fine lines over time
  • Maintains results between professional clinic sessions

What's Included

Derma Stamp device with 0.5mm titanium microneedles. Protective cap and instruction guide. Sterilisation instructions included. Replacement heads available separately — contact us via WhatsApp.

How to Use

  • Cleanse and dry skin thoroughly before use
  • Apply your chosen That Serum serum to the target area
  • Stamp gently — do not drag or press hard
  • Apply additional serum after stamping for absorption
  • Use no more than once every 21 days on any given area
  • Sterilise the stamp with 70% isopropyl alcohol before and after

Who We Are

Face Needling is a skincare sanctuary devoted to innovative, customised treatments that elevate your natural beauty and promote healthy, glowing skin. Our mission revolves around understanding and addressing individual skin concerns — ensuring every client leaves feeling revitalised and confident.

The Journey

How We Got Here

2016

The Beginning — UrbanPiercingz

When we first opened our doors in 2016, we operated under the name URBANPIERCINGZ, offering a singular focus on piercings. In our early days, we provided personalised service primarily through house calls, ensuring a convenient and intimate experience for our clients. This initial phase allowed us to cultivate a strong relationship with our community, laying the groundwork for all future expansions.

2017

Expanding — Tattoo Removal & Microneedling

The year 2017 marked a pivotal moment for our brand as we boldly expanded into tattoo removal — inspired by nearly two years of hands-on experience and clients frequently seeking solutions for their skin concerns. By the end of that year, we excitedly introduced microneedling to our repertoire. This advancement was driven by our determination to meet the diverse needs of our clientele and our commitment to offering effective treatments for various skin concerns.

2018

Microneedling Becomes Our Signature

By 2018, we experienced a dramatic shift as microneedling surged in popularity, eclipsing both our piercing and tattoo removal services. The remarkable transformations our clients underwent — revitalised skin texture, a radiant complexion, restored confidence — quickly captured widespread attention. This newfound demand propelled us to focus primarily on facial treatments, deepening our dedication to skin health and appearance.

2019

Growth & Ebony Face Beauty

2019 was a year of growth and experimentation. We ventured into stretch mark removal — eager to broaden our offerings — though we encountered challenges with the traditional microneedling pen, which proved time-consuming and limited in delivering the rapid results our clients desired. This realisation prompted a thorough reassessment of our approach. In response, we launched EBONY FACE BEAUTY, a brand featuring luxurious face masks and a specialised serum, carefully formulated for luminous and rejuvenated skin.

2020

Navigating COVID — Urban Face Therapy

The onset of the COVID-19 pandemic was a significant turning point. We rebranded to URBAN FACE THERAPY, reflecting our renewed dedication to advanced skincare as our primary focus. While the pandemic led to the discontinuation of our face mask collection, we remained operational with renewed determination. The challenges of that period only strengthened our resolve — God's grace served as our guiding light, helping us endure and emerge stronger than ever.

2022+

RF Microneedling — Raising the Bar

We expanded our microneedling packages to cover a wider variety of skin concerns and goals, maximising treatment outcomes for every client. Having perfected facial microneedling, we introduced advanced radiofrequency (RF) microneedling — dramatically enhancing our capabilities for stretch mark removal and other skin concerns. This technology combines microneedling with RF energy for deeper correction, stronger collagen stimulation, and longer-lasting results.

2024

Face Needling & That Serum

This evolution prompted us to refine our brand to FACE-NEEDLING, reflecting our focus on a broader scope of microneedling for both face and body. We also unveiled our own premium serum line — THAT SERUM — a carefully curated collection designed to nourish and rejuvenate your skin, delivering high-quality results. Explore our range at thatserum.com.

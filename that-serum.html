Our Story

Born from the Clinic

The business began in April 2016 as Urban Face Therapy — delivering over 20,000 facial treatment sessions before evolving into Face Needling. During those years, we watched clients leave with great results, only to undo their progress with products that were never designed for darker complexions: harsh brighteners, fragrance-heavy formulas, diluted actives that did almost nothing.

We tried face masks in 2019. Then COVID disrupted everything. By 2022, after years of watching what actually worked in treatment — and what didn't — we pivoted to serums. Every formula was developed alongside our clinic protocols, tested on our clients, and refined based on what we actually saw on real skin.

Today, That Serum is the at-home extension of what we do in the clinic — bridging the gap between your sessions and your daily routine so your results are maintained, protected, and built upon.

Our Philosophy

What Sets That Serum Apart

01

High-Concentration Actives

Most over-the-counter serums are diluted to avoid sensitivity — but that means they rarely do much. Our formulas use the concentrations that clinical studies support. You are getting ingredients that actually perform.

02

Melanated Skin First

Darker skin tones are more prone to post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation, more reactive to certain acids, and more likely to be over-treated by generic protocols. Every That Serum formula is developed with this in mind — safe, effective, and tested for your complexion.

03

Clinic-Aligned Formulas

Our serums are designed to work alongside — not in competition with — your clinic treatments. The right serum before a session prepares the skin. The right serum after a session supports recovery. We know which products to pair with which treatments because we designed both.

Treatments + Serums

How It Works with Your Clinic Sessions

Before

Prepare the Skin

In the weeks before your treatment, consistent use of Hyaluronic Acid or Anti-Melanin serums builds a healthier baseline — improving how your skin responds during the session.

After

Support Recovery

Post-treatment skin is at its most receptive. Hyaluronic Acid and Snail Peptide serums accelerate healing, restore moisture, and protect the barrier while new collagen forms.

Between

Maintain Results

Between sessions, active serums like Tranexamic Acid and Coenzyme Q10 continue working on pigmentation and collagen — keeping you progressing even on the days you're not in the clinic.
